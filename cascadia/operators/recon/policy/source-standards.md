# Recon Worker — Source Standards

## Trusted Sources (high confidence)
- Company official websites (.com, .org, .gov)
- LinkedIn profiles and company pages
- Industry directories (Thomasnet, ZoomInfo public, Clutch, G2)
- Government databases and public records
- News articles from established outlets
- Conference speaker bios and event pages
- Press releases and official announcements
- Chamber of commerce directories
- SEC filings and public business registrations

## Medium Confidence Sources
- Wikipedia (for context only, not as a primary data source)
- Crunchbase (funding data reliable, contact data treat as medium)
- Industry blogs and trade publications
- Job postings (confirms role exists, not contact info)
- Google Maps business listings
- Yelp and review platforms (business info only)

## Low Confidence / Use with Caution
- Reddit, Quora, forum posts
- Aggregator sites that scrape other sources
- Personal social media (Twitter/X, Facebook)
- Unverified directories and lead-gen sites
- AI-generated content pages
- Pages with no authorship or publication date

## Excluded Sources
- Paywalled content where data cannot be confirmed
- Sites that explicitly prohibit scraping in robots.txt
- Dark web or legally ambiguous data sources
- Spam-flagged domains
